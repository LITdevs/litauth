LITauth Privacy Policy

Welcome to LITauth! We will refer to this as our "Service".
This service is operated by LIT Devs ("us", "we") for users of the service ("you"). Usage of the Service is governed by our Terms of Service.
LIT Devs is not affiliated with any company.

Data we collect

We collect the following data required to operate the service:
  • Account ID, username, password (hashed and salted) and e-mail;
  • Session ID, stored on your computer via cookies;
  • OAuth applications created by you;

Third-party services

We use Cloudflare to protect our service from attacks, so you are subject to their privacy policy as well. You are a "End User" in that document.
We do not store any cookies other than Session IDs, and we do not embed tracking or analytics code in the Service.

How we use your data

Your Account ID, Session ID, and username is used to identify you. Your Account ID may be logged to us if it is detected that your account is bypassing restrictions set by us.
Your e-mail is used to identify you in support requests and send you important notifications.
We monitor OAuth applications created by you, to ensure that they are not used for malicious purposes.
Your data may be shared with a third-party application, if you consent to that via the OAuth prompt, which informs you what data is shared if you accept.

Everything we collect is stored on our servers and is only used for service operation.
We do not have advertising partners, and we do not give your data to any third parties without your consent.

Your rights

No matter where you are, we at LIT Devs believe you should have control over your data. You can:
  • View your data, as stored on our servers, by going to https://auth.litdevs.org/info.
  • Update your data by going to your profile page and clicking on your username or profile picture. To change your e-mail, you must contact us via email.
  • Delete your data by contacting us via email.

Children

Children under the age of 13 are not allowed to use the Service, even with parental permission.
If you believe that a child is using the Service, please contact us so we can remove their data from the Service.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.

Contact information
